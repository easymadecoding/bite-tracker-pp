Common Questions

  • Premium not unlocking? Tap "Restore Purchases" inside the paywall.
  • How do I cancel? Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone.
  • App crashing? Make sure you’re on the latest version and restart your device.

When contacting support, include:

  • Your iPhone model
  • Your iOS version
  • App version (if known)
  • Description of the issue
